About Weston.

Originally from Los Angeles, CA, I bring the dynamic energy of the West Coast to everything I do. Raised as the middle child in a nurturing family, I've learned the importance of gratitude, balance, and drive. Now, as a senior at the University of Iowa's Tippie College of Business, I am completing my degree in Business Analytics and Information Systems with a minor in Sport and Recreation Management.

At the heart of my academic and professional journey lies a deep-seated passion for analytics, not just as a field of study, but as a powerful tool to drive innovation and efficiency in both business and sports. My fascination with analytics began with understanding its capacity to transform complex data into actionable insights, influencing decisions that propel businesses forward. This fascination quickly extended into the realm of sports, where analytics can be a game-changer—literally.

Outside of my academic and professional pursuits, I am passionately engaged in photography, music, and sports, which provide me with a rich source of inspiration and relaxation. My love for photography allows me to capture and share the fleeting moments and stunning visuals that life offers, while music enriches my life, offering both a creative outlet and a profound way to connect with others. As an avid sports enthusiast, I not only participate in athletic activities but also deeply enjoy the strategic and analytical aspects they entail.

Portfolio

Explore a collection of projects showcasing expertise in business analytics and sports analytics. Each project illustrates advanced data processing techniques and insights derived from complex datasets, aiming to enhance strategic decisions in business environments and sports management.

April 2024

Predictive Engagement Risk Model for Healthcare Services

This University of Iowa Capstone project for AArete, a global consulting firm, develops a predictive model using claims, care gaps, and demographic data to assess engagement risk levels for Medicare members over 65, aiming to improve healthcare service delivery. The outcome includes a visual dashboard that provides stakeholders with a scoring system to predict patient visits for preventive care, thus enabling more effective healthcare management strategies.

April 2024 (In Progress)

Iowa Football Team Running Back Analytics

This ongoing project focuses on developing analytics solutions to enhance running back performance analysis. I am currently collecting and analyzing player performance data to build predictive models that assess physical metrics and in-game statistics.

March 2023

NBA MVP and Championship Analysis

This project analyzed NBA player statistics to predict the Most Valuable Player (MVP) award and identify key team statistics that contribute to championship success. By examining MVP criteria and historical data of winning teams, the project provided insights into factors that significantly influence individual and team performance, culminating in a detailed report and predictive models that aid stakeholders in strategic decision-making.
